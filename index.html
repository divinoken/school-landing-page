About IHMA

Best school to develop your child's uniqueness

IHMA is a dedicated learning institution committed to providing holistic formation and academic excellence. We believe that every student is unique, and to develop that uniqueness into bright outcomes, they need the best education.

With years of experience in providing quality education, we have nurtured students to become successful and happy individuals. We open doors to many prestigious higher education institutions.

🏆
Academic Excellence

Our PAASCU-accredited curriculum provides a high-standard education that opens doors to prestigious higher education institutions. We empower students through innovative, competitive, and globally recognized learning programs.

💙
The Blue Identity

Rooted in our devotion to the Immaculate Heart of Mary, we take pride in a tradition that fosters a strong sense of belonging. Our students carry a legacy of poise and excellence that is recognized throughout the community.

🔍
Value-Based Discipline

We believe in holistic growth where students are monitored closely and guided by Christian values. We maintain constant communication with parents to ensure students are nurtured into disciplined, successful, and happy individuals.

Vision

Mission
